Light Life

Improve your health condition
in such a easy way

Introduction

Light life is a mobile application which helps busy people in modern society relieved from various ailments. By encouraging users to develop good habits, they may start leading healthy lives even though tasks customised for them only takes them a little time a day.

According to a health report conducted by Tencent, 77% of the employee suffer from cervical spondylopathy, and 71% of them suffer from ametropia.

Background

The poster left illustrates that white collar officers now face ailments usually ignored by them, which inspired me to develop tools to help them.

Persona

Wang Ying is a 38-year-old architect. She has a son who goes to middle school. Because of the work requirement, she has to face computers every day. As a result, her cervical bone is injured. Although she complains about it a lot, she seldom actually do something to improve health because the measures she found on the Internet looks too complicated and she is reluctant to pay any time or energy to take them.

Generalization of User Research

It is not easy to orgnaize logically from the conversations with users. But they seem have many similar difficulities: limited time, limited energy and a little bit laziness after work. Most importantly, they do not see it as a big deal if it is not serious.
They want something small and delicated, helpful and customized. The product designed for them is demanding, since it is supposed to not only form the habits to lead a healthy life, but also require users' efforts as few as possible.
Since to form a habit is so hard, why not encourage users by gamification? This idea is formed.
